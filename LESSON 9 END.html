Leveraging Campaigns in Jasper AI
Another cool function for personalization in Jasper AI is the ability to create whole marketing campaigns. This function allows you to manage and generate a series of related content pieces, ensuring consistency across all your marketing materials.
How do you think you should start when creating a new campaign in Jasper AI?
Incorrect answer

Not quite. The first step is to create a new campaign.

After you create a new campaign in Jasper AI, what do you think comes next?
Correct answer

After creating the campaign, you should enter the campaign name manually.

What will you do next after entering the campaign name in Jasper AI?
Correct answer

Correct! After entering the campaign name, you should select the campaign voice (TOV).

Well done! After all these steps, you are ready to copy and paste the campaign summary.
Selecting Campaign Assets
Once you've set up your campaign, start selecting the assets you need. Jasper AI offers a variety of assets, including: - Blog post - Case study - Facebook image ad - Landing page - Nurture email sequence - Promotional email sequence - TikTok script - Twitter thread - Press release
Generating Campaign Assets
After selecting your assets, click "Generate," and in just a few seconds, Jasper AI will build a full campaign asset gallery for you.
With Jasper AI, creating personalized content and comprehensive marketing campaigns is quick and easy. You'll save time and ensure consistency across all your content.
